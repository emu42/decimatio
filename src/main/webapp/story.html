Welcome to the Roman Legion!

After a relentless battle against the proud kingdom of Gamebertia, you find yourself unable to conquer their heavily guarded fortress. You were about to rise in the ranks, as your majestic blue garment signals.

 

 

The other legionnaires, wearing white garments, were looking up to you, hoping you would lead them to victory. Seeing that the situation is hopeless, you decide to turn around and go back home. As a consequence, you and your troops have to face your punishment:

DECIMATIO !

 

You feel sorry for the other legionnaires, except for one of them. The man rivalling you in getting the promotion you’ve been longing for. You cannot stand him and he smells a little. Probably the reason his clothes yellowed a bit. Of course, he enjoys every moment of letting you know what a failure of a mission you were leading. You wouldn’t mind this guy falling victim to the punishment. There is no escape.
